About

I build things to understand them.

Away from the workbench I'm usually deep in science fiction — Liu Cixin's Three-Body Problem trilogy quietly rearranged how I think about scale, time, and how fragile a civilisation can be.

I like systems and order: things in their right place, named well, easy to reason about. The same instinct follows me into music and film. I go out of my way for good math, alternative, and progressive rock, and for things worth watching closely — with a real IMAX here in Prague, I'm in it for the camera as much as the story: a great lens and a plot that earns its runtime, in films and series alike.
